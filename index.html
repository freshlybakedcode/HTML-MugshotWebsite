mugshot

we'll have a tea, please.

A solution to the problem of carrying two fistfuls of mugs to the kitchen and then trying to work out which drink goes where.

Whip out your phone and use the app to snap a quick picture of the relevant drinking vestibule (a mugshot, if you will), and then choose the drink and milk/sugar requirements. You'll get a list of pictures and the relevant drinks next to them to take away the stress and guesswork associated with the tea round chore!

Surprise and delight your co-workers with perfect drinks every time!
